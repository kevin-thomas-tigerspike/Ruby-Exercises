Exercise 8: Anagram Detection

Your Task

An anagram is the result of rearranging the letters of a word to produce a new word. (Ref wikipedia).

Note: anagrams are case and punctuation insensitive
Examples
  • Creative is an anagram of Reactive
  • Buckethead is an anagram of Death Cube K
  • Edward Gorey is an anagram of E. G. Deadworry
